# 📜 Munaliiga Rules & Guidelines

> [!info]
> These rules are designed to ensure a **fair, beginner-friendly, and enjoyable experience** for all participants.

---

## 🧭 1. Code of Conduct

Munaliiga is built around a few core principles:

- ✅ **Respect and good behavior are mandatory**
- 🎯 **The primary goal is to learn and enjoy the game**
- 🤝 **Everyone contributes to a positive environment**

> [!tip]
> Friendly banter is welcome — as long as it stays respectful and in good spirit.

> [!warning]
> Disruptive, toxic, or inappropriate behavior should be reported immediately.  
> Violations may result in:
> - Warning  
> - Match penalties  
> - Removal from the league  

---

## 🎮 2. Match Rules

> [!info]
> **Official matches** = Any scheduled league games that are part of the current format  
> (e.g. round-robin matches or playoffs)

- 🎯 Game mode: **Captain’s Mode**
- 🧠 **Coaches handle drafting and strategy**
- 🕹️ A player executes the draft in-game (acts as captain)
- 🔇 After drafting, **coaches must leave voice chat**

---

## ⚖️ 3. Competitive Integrity

To keep matches fair and balanced:

- 👤 Play only on **your own account**
- 📊 **Public match data must be enabled**
- 🚫 No unfair advantages:
  - Smurfing  
  - Account sharing  
  - Outside assistance during matches  

---

## 🎓 4. Coaching Guidelines

- 🧑‍🏫 Coaches are here to **support, guide, and teach**
- ⏱️ Coaching during matches is allowed **only during drafting**
- 💬 Outside matches, coaching is encouraged  
  - (as long as it does not harm match balance)

---

## 💬 5. Communication

- 🗣️ Communicate **constructively** with your team
- 💡 Feedback and discussion are encouraged
- 🤝 Keep communication **respectful and supportive**

> [!tip]
> A good team environment makes learning faster and more enjoyable for everyone.

---

## 📅 6. Participation

> [!info]
> Munaliiga is designed to be **low-threshold and accessible**

- 👍 Players are encouraged to **show up to scheduled matches**
- 📢 Inform your team if you cannot attend

> [!note]
> This is not about strict enforcement —  
> it's about ensuring a smooth and enjoyable experience for everyone.

---

## 🧑‍⚖️ 7. Organizer Decisions

- ⚖️ The organizer has final say on:
  - Rule enforcement  
  - Player eligibility  
  - Conflict resolution  

- 🧩 Situations not covered by rules are handled:
  - Case-by-case  
  - With fairness and player experience as priority  

---

## 💡 8. General Tips

> [!warning]
> If you live with a significantly more experienced Dota player,  
> **do not play on the same computer**

- ⚙️ Dota’s smurf detection can be unpredictable  
- 📉 This may lead to unbalanced and unenjoyable matches  

✅ Always:
- Use your **own account**  
- Play on your **own machine**

> [!tip]
> Avoid full party games with coaches —  
> this can negatively affect match quality.
