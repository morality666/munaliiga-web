# About Munaliiga

Munaliiga is a beginner-friendly Dota 2 league built around learning, teamwork, and a low-pressure competitive experience.

---

## 1. What Munaliiga Is

Have you ever watched your friends or people close to you play Dota 2 and thought:

> I would like to try it, but I do not know where to start. It looks incredibly difficult, and the learning curve must be overwhelming.

Munaliiga is designed exactly for that. It offers a low-threshold way to explore the competitive side of Dota 2.

Munaliiga is community-driven and aimed at providing new and returning players with a safe and encouraging environment to learn the game and experience team-based play.

---

## 2. Teams and Coaching

The league brings together first-time players and beginners, forming balanced teams guided by experienced coaches.

Under their guidance, players can begin exploring the depth and magic of Dota.

> [!tip]
> The goal is not to be perfect from day one. The goal is to learn the game with a team around you.

---

## 3. Team Balance

Teams are built to be as balanced as possible to ensure fair and enjoyable matches for all participants.

Balancing is based on:

- Information provided in the registration form
- Possible follow-up interviews
- Pre-season test matches
- Player preferences for teammates

The primary focus is learning, teamwork, and creating a positive playing experience.

---

## 4. Experienced Players

Any more experienced applicants are evaluated on a case-by-case basis through the registration process to ensure the league remains balanced and beginner-friendly.

A small amount of experience within each team is seen as a valuable asset and is used accordingly.
