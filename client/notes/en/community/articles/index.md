# Articles

This section is for match reports, player stories, interviews and other writing from the Munaliiga community.

## Contributing

Send your idea or draft to the organisers on Discord. Plain Markdown is ideal, but a normal text document is fine too. Include a title, the name you want credited and any images you have permission to publish.
